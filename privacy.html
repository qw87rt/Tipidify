Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Tipidify (“the App”) handles information when you use the application.

2. Information We Collect

Tipidify does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All data entered into the App (such as income and expense records) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any external servers.

3. How We Use Information

Any data entered into the App is used solely to:

  • Track income and expenses
  • Calculate totals and summaries
  • Display information within the App

This data never leaves your device and is not accessed by the developer.

4. Third-Party Services

Tipidify does not use any third-party services that collect user data, including analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data stored locally on your device. However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Tipidify does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page and/or within the App.
